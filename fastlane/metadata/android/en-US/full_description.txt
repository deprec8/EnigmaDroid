<p><strong>EnigmaDroid</strong> is a modern, user-friendly app that lets you control your Enigma2-based satellite or cable set-top box directly from your Android device.</p><p>Everywhere in your home, <strong>EnigmaDroid</strong> gives you full access to your receiver’s features and content.</p><h3>Features</h3><ul><li><strong>Channel & Bouquet List</strong>: Quickly navigate your full channel lineup and favorite bouquets..</li><li><strong>Timer Management</strong>: Add, remove or edit timers and scheduled recordings.</li><li><strong>Stream Recordings</strong>: Browse and play your saved shows and movies.</li><li><strong>EPG Support</strong>: View Electronic Program Guide data with detailed show info for every bouquet.</li><li><strong>Remote Control</strong>: Full on-screen remote control with keypad.</li><li><strong>Live TV Streaming</strong>: Watch live broadcasts from your Enigma2 box on your device using a compatible video player.</li><li><strong>Search & Filter</strong>: Find channels or programs with built-in search functionality on every page.</li><li><strong>Multi-Box Support</strong>: Connect to multiple receivers and switch between them with ease.</li></ul><h3>Streaming</h3><p><strong>EnigmaDroid</strong> currently uses external media players for streaming live TV and recordings.</p><p><strong>VLC</strong> is recommended, though other media players may also work.</p><h3>Requirements</h3><ul><li>An <strong>Enigma2-based</strong> set-top box (satellite or cable).</li><li>Up-to-date <strong>OpenWebif</strong> plugin enabled and configured.</li></ul><h3>Compatibility</h3><p>Tested with popular Enigma2 images.</p><p>Some images, like <strong>VTi</strong>, may require manually updating the OpenWebif plugin to ensure full compatibility.</p><h3>Free & Open Source</h3><p>EnigmaDroid is fully open-source, respects your privacy and contains no ads or trackers. Contributions and feature requests are welcome on the project's GitHub repository.</p>
