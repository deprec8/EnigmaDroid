<strong>EnigmaDroid</strong> is a modern, user-friendly app that lets you control your Enigma2-based satellite or cable set-top box directly from your Android device.

Everywhere in your home, <strong>EnigmaDroid</strong> gives you full access to your receiver’s features and content.

<strong>Features</strong>
<ul>
<li><strong>Channel & Bouquet List</strong>: Quickly navigate your full channel lineup and favorite bouquets.</li>
<li><strong>Timer Management</strong>: Add, remove or edit timers and scheduled recordings.</li>
<li><strong>Stream Recordings</strong>: Browse and play your saved shows and movies.</li>
<li><strong>EPG Support</strong>: View Electronic Program Guide data with detailed show info for every bouquet.</li>
<li><strong>Remote Control</strong>: Full on-screen remote control with keypad.</li>
<li><strong>Live TV Streaming</strong>: Watch live broadcasts from your Enigma2 box on your device using a compatible video player.</li>
<li><strong>Search & Filter</strong>: Find channels or programs with built-in search functionality on every page.</li>
<li><strong>Multi-Box Support</strong>: Connect to multiple receivers and switch between them with ease.</li>
</ul>
<strong>Streaming</strong>

<strong>EnigmaDroid</strong> currently uses external media players for streaming live TV and recordings.

<strong>VLC</strong> is recommended, though other media players may also work.

<strong>Requirements</strong>
<ul>
<li>An <strong>Enigma2-based</strong> set-top box (satellite or cable).</li>
<li>Up-to-date <strong>OpenWebif</strong> plugin enabled and configured.</li>
</ul>
<strong>Compatibility</strong>

Tested with popular Enigma2 images.

Some images, like <strong>VTi</strong>, may require manually updating the OpenWebif plugin to ensure full compatibility.

<strong>Free & Open Source</strong>

EnigmaDroid is fully open-source, respects your privacy and contains no ads or trackers. Contributions and feature requests are welcome on the project's GitHub repository.
